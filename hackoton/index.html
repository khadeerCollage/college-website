RGUKT (Rajiv Gandhi University of Knowledge Technologies) is a state university in Andhra Pradesh,
India that offers a six-year integrated B. Tech. degree program.

RGUKT was established in March 2008 by the AP Government with three campuses through an Act of the Legislature. The first batch of about 6,500 tenth class graduates
was admitted into the six year integrated program in August of 2008.
The initial selection was based on the local best model where students receiving the top marks in every subject were selected.
